About Ghangaria Inn

Your Comfortable Base Camp for Valley of Flowers & Hemkund Sahib

Experience Warm Himalayan Hospitality

Located near the Gurudwara in Ghangaria, Ghangaria Inn is a comfortable stay for trekkers, pilgrims, families, and nature lovers visiting the Valley of Flowers National Park and Hemkund Sahib.

We provide clean rooms, 24-hour hot water, delicious home-style food, friendly service, and a peaceful environment surrounded by the beautiful Himalayan mountains.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your trek

Electricity in Ghangaria can be intermittent due to weather. We recommend carrying power banks. BSNL and Jio networks work in patches, but bandwidth is highly limited.

Ghangaria Inn is accessible via a beautiful 14 km trek from Govindghat/Pulna. You can cover this distance by trekking, hiring a pony/porter, or taking a helicopter ride to the Ghangaria helipad.

The Valley of Flowers National Park opens from June 1st to October 31st. The absolute best time to see maximum floral blooms is between July and September.
